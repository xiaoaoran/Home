Aoran Xiao (肖傲然)

I am a Ph.D. student at School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCSE), Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, where I work on deep learning, point cloud understanding, and computer vision, etc, supervised by Prof. Shijian Lu. Before that, I got my Master degree at LIESMARS of Wuhan University, China, where I was co-advised by Prof. Deren Li and Prof. Ruizhi Chen. I did my bachelors at School of Remote Sensing and Information Engineering, Wuhan University.
